I’m driven by a desire to solve real-world problems, and I enjoy projects that challenge me to integrate APIs and create seamless user experiences. I’m particularly skilled at bridging the gap between users and technology by making complex systems intuitive and easy to use.

Projects:

It uses a real-time location feature and an interactive body diagram to provide doctors with valuable information, improving healthcare response times and outcomes.

It integrates with Google Places and provides real-time location suggestions, ensuring a smooth and satisfying user experience.
